    A LITTLE UNIVERSITY PROJECT

    This site was made by the end of taking a good note in the discipline of "Hypermedia desing ", with the Purpose of making this site in representation to a topic, in this case, about a country.


    Who I Am?


         I am a student of CTESP in Tecnologies and Programming about Informating's Systems, that made this site as a project of a discipline called "Design Hypermédia", whose goal was to create a website about a country. My work was dealt with in order to create a tourist guide site on my topic: Venezuela, explaining a little about each important aspect of the country (its culture and traditions, food and main tourist destinations).

         As a student from Venezuela, I wanted to create a tourist site in Venezuela in the most complex and complete way possible, but the lack of knowledge in various techniques about HTML limited my creativity, and also that the lack of time is an enemy that is always Complicate things when a student wants to do everything in the best possible way, however, I always tried that the site had the same structure and that, at the same time, is pleasing in the view of the user, point that is very important. The site has a very simple structure, but with all the view, so that navigation by it is not a complicated task and at the same time is very fun and comfortable.

         On this site I tried to explain and show the best of Venezuela, a task in which I feel not very satisfied, all by the fact that there are so many things that by not having enough time I can not talk about everything, but I tried to show and talk about everything the most important of More concrete, complete and fast as possible, so that anyone who has a good idea of Venezuela and its culture (without having spoken about the traditions of the bagpipes and what we do in the Christmas season... Which is when we usually eat hallacas and ham bread while we listen or sing the Gaitas, the natural musical genre of Maracaibo).

         With all this explained, I hope you enjoyed the visit to the site and that you have learned something from Venezuela, and that the teacher also so Cosneguir take a 20 in the discipline. For more information about me, my social networks are at the end of the site!
